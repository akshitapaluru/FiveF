Family

Family is everything!

Family are the people you look up to from young age.
They are usually the first people you meet and recognize.
My family is made out of four people including me.
My mom, My dad, my little brother and me.
We all are very close and have great connections with each other.
I especially like playing with my brother and he enjoys my company too.
Me and my brother have an eight year age difference but we get along very well.
I have a great connection with my parents as well. I share almost everything with them.
In my world, my family is one of the most important thigns in my life.
